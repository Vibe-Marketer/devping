## Longer launch post

I just launched DevPing.

DevPing is a native macOS notification utility for AI coding tools. It helps when you’re running long prompts, stepping away from the terminal, or switching contexts and don’t want to keep checking whether your assistant is done.

What it does:
- completion alerts
- permission-needed alerts
- focus-aware behavior
- one-click return to the right editor or terminal
- native settings for sound, timing, and appearance

The direct-download launch build is signed and notarized, and the core product does not require an account.

- Site: https://devping.app
